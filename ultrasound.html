Diagnostic Imaging

At LifePoint Medical Centre, we offer advanced diagnostic imaging services that play a crucial role in identifying and managing various medical conditions. Our cutting-edge imaging technology ensures that healthcare providers have access to detailed and accurate images for faster, more effective diagnosis and treatment planning.

Our Diagnostic Imaging Services Include

  • MRI
  • (CT) Scan
  • Ultrasound
  • X-Ray
Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)

Our MRI services provide high-definition images of the body’s organs, tissues, and bones. MRI is especially valuable for diagnosing conditions related to the brain, spine, joints, and soft tissues without the need for radiation.
Book Now

Computed Tomography (CT) Scan

CT scans use X-rays to create cross-sectional images of the body, offering more detail than standard X-rays. This imaging technique is crucial for diagnosing injuries, tumors, infections, and internal bleeding, providing essential information for surgical planning.
Book Now

Ultrasound

Ultrasound imaging uses sound waves to generate real-time images of the body’s internal organs and tissues. It is widely used for prenatal care, abdominal scans, and soft tissue assessments, allowing for accurate and non-invasive diagnostics.
Book Now

Digital X-Ray

Digital X-ray technology at LifePoint Medical Centre provides fast and clear imaging with minimal radiation exposure. This service is used to diagnose bone fractures, lung conditions, and other skeletal or internal abnormalities, delivering instant results for quicker diagnoses.
